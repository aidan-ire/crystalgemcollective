About this show

I'm going to show off some parts of 'Steven Universe' in this website: 'Steven Universe' is a captivating coming-of-age narrative seen through the eyes of Steven, the youngest member of the Crystal Gems, a group of mystical protectors dedicated to safeguarding the Earth. This animated series, born from Cartoon Network Studios' 'Shorts Development Initiative,' is the brainchild of Rebecca Sugar, a renowned writer and storyboard artist who made history as the network's first female solo show creator. The show follows Steven's journey as a unique gem-human hybrid adopted by his mother's friends, The Crystal Gems. Through battles against world-threatening foes, it delves into moral complexity, intense fights, and unexpected plot twists that make it truly exceptional..
